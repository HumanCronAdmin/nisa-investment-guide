## Why This Tool?

Maxed out your NISA allowance? Compare iDeCo, taxable brokerage accounts, overseas brokers, and savings bonds — see projected growth with tax impact.

## Features

- **Visual growth chart** — See how your investments could grow over time
- **4 investment options** — iDeCo, Taxable Brokerage, Overseas Brokerage, Savings & Bonds
- **Tax impact simulation** — Compare after-tax returns across options
- **Adjustable parameters** — Set monthly amount, time horizon, and risk tolerance
- **Side-by-side comparison** — Annual return, tax, and final value in one table

## How It Works

1. Set your monthly investment amount and time period
2. Adjust your risk tolerance level
3. Compare projected after-tax returns across investment options
